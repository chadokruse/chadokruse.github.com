Periscope GIS

PeriscopeGIS is an online application that intends to make large GIS datasets useful to the 80% of GIS users with little to no formal GIS training.

We're leveraging the ubiquity of Google Earth™, the cost-effectiveness of AWS, and a little proprietary magic to drastically improve the user experience when accessing large, complicated GIS datasets.

Largely a "nights and weekends" project.
